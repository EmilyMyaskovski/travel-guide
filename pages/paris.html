Travel Guide to Tokyo, Japan

Recommended Attractions

  • Tokyo Skytree

    Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world, standing at 634 meters tall. From its observation decks, you can enjoy breathtaking views of the city.

    View on Google Maps

  • Shibuya Crossing

    Shibuya Crossing is one of the busiest intersections in the world, surrounded by neon lights and giant video screens. It's a must-see for anyone visiting Tokyo.

    View on Google Maps

  • Meiji Shrine

    Meiji Shrine is a serene Shinto shrine located in a peaceful forested area, dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken.

    View on Google Maps
