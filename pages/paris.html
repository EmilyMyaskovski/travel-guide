Travel Guide to Tokyo, Japan

Recommended Attractions

  • Shibuya Crossing

    One of the busiest and most famous intersections in the world, Shibuya Crossing is a must-visit for any Tokyo traveler. Take in the sights and sounds of this iconic spot, surrounded by neon lights and giant video screens.

    View on Google Maps

  • Meiji Shrine

    Located in a serene forested area, Meiji Shrine is a beautiful Shinto shrine dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken. Take a peaceful stroll through the gardens and grounds, and learn about the shrine's history and significance.

    View on Google Maps

  • Tsukiji Fish Market

    While the inner market has moved to a new location, the outer market still offers a fascinating glimpse into Tokyo's seafood culture. Browse the stalls, try some fresh sushi, and take in the bustling atmosphere of this iconic market.

    View on Google Maps
