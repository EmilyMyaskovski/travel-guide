Paris Travel Guide

Recommended Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower

    The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. Take the elevator to the top for breathtaking views of the city.

    View on Google Maps

  • Louvre Museum

    The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts from ancient civilizations to the 19th century, including the Mona Lisa.

    View on Google Maps

  • Arc de Triomphe

    The Arc de Triomphe is a monumental arch that honors the soldiers who fought and died for France, offering stunning views of the Champs-Élysées and the city from its top.

    View on Google Maps
