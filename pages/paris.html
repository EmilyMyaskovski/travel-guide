Paris Travel Guide

Recommended Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower

    The iconic Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city from its top deck.

    View on Google Maps

  • Louvre Museum

    The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts from ancient civilizations to the 19th century.

    View on Google Maps

  • Montmartre

    Montmartre is a charming and historic neighborhood in Paris, known for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and stunning views of the city from its highest point, the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

    View on Google Maps
