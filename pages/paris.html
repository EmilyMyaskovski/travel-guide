Travel Guide to Tokyo, Japan

Recommended Attractions

  • Shibuya Crossing

    Shibuya Crossing is one of the busiest and most iconic intersections in the world, surrounded by neon lights, giant video screens, and hundreds of pedestrians crossing at the same time. It's a must-see for any Tokyo visitor.

    View on Google Maps

  • Tokyo Skytree

    Tokyo Skytree is a 2,040-foot-tall broadcasting tower that offers stunning views of the city from its observation decks. On a clear day, you can even see Mount Fuji in the distance.

    View on Google Maps

  • Meguro River

    Meguro River is a scenic river that runs through the heart of Tokyo and is lined with cherry blossom trees, cafes, and shops. It's a popular spot for strolling and taking in the city's atmosphere.

    View on Google Maps
