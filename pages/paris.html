Bangkok Travel Guide

Recommended Attractions

  • The Grand Palace

    The Grand Palace is one of the most iconic landmarks in Bangkok, serving as the official residence of the king of Thailand. The palace's stunning architecture and beautiful gardens make it a must-visit attraction.

    View on Google Maps

  • Wat Phra Kaew

    Wat Phra Kaew is a beautiful temple complex located within the grounds of the Grand Palace, housing the famous Emerald Buddha. The temple's intricate architecture and stunning murals make it a must-visit attraction.

    View on Google Maps

  • Chatuchak Weekend Market

    Chatuchak Weekend Market is one of the largest markets in Asia, with over 15,000 stalls selling everything from clothing and accessories to souvenirs and street food. The market is a great place to experience the local culture and try some delicious Thai cuisine.

    View on Google Maps
