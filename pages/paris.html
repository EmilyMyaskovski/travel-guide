Paris Travel Guide

Top Attractions

Notre-Dame Cathedral

One of the most famous landmarks in Paris, Notre-Dame Cathedral is a beautiful example of Gothic architecture. Take a guided tour to explore its stunning stained glass windows and intricate stone carvings.

View on Google Maps

The Louvre Museum

The world-renowned Louvre Museum is home to an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the famous Mona Lisa painting. Explore the museum's grand halls and discover the beauty of the past.

View on Google Maps

Eiffel Tower

The iconic Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris. Take the elevator to the top for breathtaking views of the city, and enjoy a romantic dinner at one of the tower's fine dining restaurants.
