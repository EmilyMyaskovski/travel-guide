Paris Travel Guide

Top Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower

    The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. Take the elevator to the top for breathtaking views of the city.

    View on Google Maps

  • Louvre Museum

    The Louvre Museum is home to an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the Mona Lisa. The museum's stunning glass pyramid entrance is a must-see.

    View on Google Maps

  • Notre Dame Cathedral

    Notre Dame Cathedral is a beautiful and historic church that dates back to the 12th century. The cathedral's stunning Gothic architecture and beautiful stained-glass windows make it a must-visit.

    View on Google Maps
