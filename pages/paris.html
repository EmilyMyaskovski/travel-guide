Travel Guide to Tokyo, Japan

Recommended Attractions

Shibuya Crossing Shibuya Crossing is one of the busiest and most iconic intersections in the world, surrounded by neon lights, giant video screens, and trendy fashion stores. Take a stroll around the Shibuya area to experience the city's vibrant energy.

View on Google Maps

Tsukiji Fish Market Tsukiji Fish Market is one of the largest fish markets in the world, offering a fascinating glimpse into Tokyo's seafood culture. Try some of the freshest sushi you've ever had at one of the many restaurants in the area.

View on Google Maps

Akihabara Akihabara is Tokyo's electronics and anime hub, with streets lined with shops selling everything from gadgets to video games. Explore the unique culture and find rare treasures among the crowded alleys.
