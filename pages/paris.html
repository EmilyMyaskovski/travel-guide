Here is the travel guide for Paris:

Must-See Attractions in Paris

Notre-Dame Cathedral

This beautiful Gothic cathedral is one of the most iconic landmarks in Paris and a must-visit for any traveler.

View on Google Maps

The Louvre Museum

One of the world's largest and most famous museums, the Louvre is home to an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world, including the Mona Lisa.

View on Google Maps

Montmartre District

This charming and historic neighborhood is known for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and stunning views of the city from the top of the hill.
