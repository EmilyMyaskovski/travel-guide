Travel Guide: Exploring Tokyo, Japan

Recommended Attractions

  • Tokyo Skytree

    Standing at 634 meters tall, Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks.

    View on Google Maps

  • Meiji Shrine

    Located in a peaceful forested area, Meiji Shrine is a serene Shinto shrine dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken.

    View on Google Maps

  • Shibuya Crossing

    One of the busiest and most iconic intersections in the world, Shibuya Crossing is a must-see for any Tokyo traveler, with its neon lights and endless stream of pedestrians.

    View on Google Maps
