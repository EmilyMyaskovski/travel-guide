Travel Guide to Tokyo, Japan

Recommended Attractions

  • Tokyo Skytree

    At 2,040 feet tall, Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks.

    View on Google Maps

  • Shibuya Crossing

    One of the busiest intersections in the world, Shibuya Crossing is a must-see for its vibrant energy and stunning views of the Tokyo skyline.

    View on Google Maps

  • Meiji Shrine

    Located in a serene forested area, Meiji Shrine is a beautiful Shinto shrine dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shoken.

    View on Google Maps
