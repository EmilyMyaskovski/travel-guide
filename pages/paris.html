Paris Travel Guide

Recommended Attractions

Eiffel Tower - The iconic Eiffel Tower is a must-visit attraction in Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks. It's an engineering marvel and a symbol of French culture.

View on Google Maps

Louvre Museum - The Louvre is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts from ancient civilizations to the 19th century. Its stunning glass pyramid entrance is a work of art in itself.

View on Google Maps

Montmartre - This charming neighborhood is famous for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and stunning views of the city from the top of the hill. Get lost in the narrow streets and discover charming cafes, shops, and historic landmarks.
