Travel Guide: Exploring the Best of Tokyo

Recommended Attractions

  • Tokyo Skytree

    At 634 meters tall, Tokyo Skytree is the tallest tower in the world, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks. Visitors can also explore the surrounding Solamachi shopping complex.

    View on Google Maps

  • Shibuya Crossing

    Shibuya Crossing is one of the busiest and most iconic intersections in the world, surrounded by neon lights and giant video screens. Take a stroll around the Shibuya area to experience the vibrant atmosphere.

    View on Google Maps

  • Meguro River

    The Meguro River runs through the heart of Tokyo, offering a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Take a stroll along the riverbank and enjoy the beautiful cherry blossom views in spring.

    View on Google Maps
