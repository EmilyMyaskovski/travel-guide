Paris Travel Guide

Recommended Attractions

Eiffel Tower - The iconic iron lady of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks. Open daily from 9:30 AM to 11:45 PM.

View on Google Maps

Louvre Museum - One of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts from ancient civilizations to the 19th century. Open daily from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, with extended hours on Wednesdays and Fridays.

View on Google Maps

Notre-Dame Cathedral - A beautiful and historic Gothic cathedral that was built in the 12th century and is one of the most famous landmarks in Paris. Open daily from 8:00 AM to 6:45 PM.

View on Google Maps
