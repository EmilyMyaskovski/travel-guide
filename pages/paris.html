Paris Travel Guide

Top Attractions

The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic iron lattice tower built for the 1889 World's Fair. It's a must-visit attraction in Paris.

The Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most visited museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral is a stunning Gothic cathedral that dates back to the 12th century. It's a beautiful and historic landmark in the heart of Paris.
